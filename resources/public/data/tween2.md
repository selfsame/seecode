## constructor args

`[target-value & unordered-opts]`

  * ```0.7``` A float - ```{:duration 0.7}```
  * ```:+``` relative flag - ```{:+ true}```
  * `:pow2` - `:pow5` easing ```{:in :pow2 :out :pow5}```
  * ```#(%)``` callback fn ```{:callback (atom [#(%)])}```
